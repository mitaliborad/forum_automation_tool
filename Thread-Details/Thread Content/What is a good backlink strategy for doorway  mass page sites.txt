Topic User: MS Shahbaz
Topic: Programmatic SEO – Create unique, valuable content per city, not just swapped names.
Localized Backlinks – Focus on citations, niche directories, and geo-specific links.
Google Business Profiles – Optimize and manage listings for each location.
Schema Markup – Implement LocalBusiness schema for better local relevance.
Content Variation – Add local testimonials, case studies, and city-specific FAQs.
Crawl Budget Optimization – Strengthen internal linking and avoid thin content.

Topic User: SaaSBacklinker
Topic: For doorway or mass page sites, a good backlink strategy involves tiered link building, where clean, contextual links go to your main pages (Tier 1), and supporting links (web 2.0s, social signals, etc.) boost those (Tier 2). Focus on relevant sources, diversify your anchor text, and avoid blasting links to just one page. Using buffer sites or authority platforms (like Medium) can help reduce risk. Keep everything looking natural and track progress closely — mass page SEO is powerful but needs smart handling.
